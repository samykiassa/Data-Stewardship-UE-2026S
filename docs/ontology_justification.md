## Onotology Justification

SNOMED CT was chosen due to its recognition as a clinical terminology standard used in healthcare and injury classification interoperability, and its widespread international use. NCIT was chosen for demographic attributes like 'age' because it offers research-friendly biomedical terminology.

The decision to choose geospatial attributes for GeoSPARQL is driven by its ability to represent spatial data semantically in a standardized way, facilitating interoperability. The date and time of the accidents were included as temporal concepts and were coded with OWL-Time. The concepts of environmental nature were selected: weather, lighting, road surface conditions were SWEET and ENVO.
